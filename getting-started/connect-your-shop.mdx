---
title: "Connect your shop"
description: "Link your Etsy, Shopify, or TikTok Shop to etSEO so it can optimize your listings."
---

Connecting your shop lets etSEO read your existing listings and write optimized content for them. It takes a few minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Connect page">
    From your etSEO dashboard, select **Connect shop**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your platform">
    Pick Etsy, Shopify, or TikTok Shop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize access">
    You will be sent to your platform to sign in and approve access. etSEO only requests the permissions it needs to read and update your listings. We never see or store your password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your shop">
    Once approved, your shop and listings appear in etSEO. You are ready to optimize.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  For Etsy, etSEO uses the official Etsy connection to identify your shop. You do not need to enter your listings by hand.
</Note>
