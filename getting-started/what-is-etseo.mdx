etSEO is an AI-powered tool that optimizes your product listings for search. Instead of guessing which keywords to use, etSEO analyzes what buyers are actually searching for and writes listing content designed to rank.

## What etSEO creates for you

- **Titles** that lead with the keywords buyers search for
- **Descriptions** that read naturally and rank well
- **Tags** that match high-traffic search terms
- **Alt text** that helps your product images get found
