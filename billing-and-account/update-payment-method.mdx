---
title: "Update your payment method"
description: "Keep your subscription active by updating your card or fixing a failed payment."
---

Keeping a valid payment method on file makes sure your etSEO subscription stays active and your listings keep getting optimized.

## Update your card

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing">
    Go to **Account, then Billing** in etSEO.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit payment method">
    Select **Update payment method** and enter your new card details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save your changes. Your next payment will use the updated card.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If a payment failed

If a charge does not go through, you will see a banner in etSEO and receive an email. To fix it:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the recovery prompt">
    Select **Update card** in the payment banner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a valid card">
    Add a working card, then retry the payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  We retry failed payments automatically for a short window, so updating your card quickly is usually all it takes to stay active.
</Note>
