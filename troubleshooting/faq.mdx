## Getting started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My shop will not connect">
    Make sure you are signed in to the correct shop account on your platform, then try connecting again. If it still fails, disconnect and reconnect from **Connect shop**, or contact support@etseo.com.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does etSEO change my listings automatically?">
    etSEO generates optimized content for you to review. You stay in control of what gets published to your shop.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which platforms does etSEO support?">
    etSEO works with Etsy, Shopify, and TikTok Shop.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My payment failed. What now?">
    Update your card from the payment banner or **Account, then Billing**. See [Update your payment method](/billing-and-account/update-payment-method).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I cancel?">
    You can cancel anytime from **Account, then Billing**. Your plan stays active until the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Results

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long until I see more traffic?">
    Search platforms take time to re-index updated listings. Many sellers start to see movement within a few weeks, though results vary by shop and category.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
