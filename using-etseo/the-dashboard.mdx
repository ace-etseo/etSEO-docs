Your dashboard is the home base for tracking how your shop is performing and what to do next.

## What you will see

- **KPI summary** shows your key numbers at a glance, including recent views and active listings.
- **Views over time** charts how your traffic is trending so you can spot momentum or dips.
- **Store health** gives you a simple read on how well your listings are optimized.
- **Top listings** ranks your best performing products so you know what is working.

## Notifications

The bell icon at the top shows alerts such as ranking changes, payment issues, and new features. Check it regularly so nothing slips by.

<Note>
  If a listing ranking starts to slip, etSEO flags it so you can refresh the content before it costs you traffic.
</Note>
